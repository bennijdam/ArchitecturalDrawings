Step 2 of 4

Tell us about your property

Your property type and designations affect your Permitted Development rights. Listed buildings and flats have different rules.

Property type
Is the property listed?
Listed buildings have no Permitted Development rights. All external and most internal alterations require Listed Building Consent and usually full planning permission.
Is it in a conservation area?
Conservation areas restrict many Permitted Development rights, particularly for extensions, dormers, and outbuildings. Side extensions, rear dormers, and cladding changes typically require planning permission.
