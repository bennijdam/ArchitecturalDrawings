What type of application are you making?

Different application routes have different statutory determination periods. Select whichever fits your project best.

Householder application
Extensions, lofts, dormers — 8-week statutory determination.
Full planning permission
New build, major works, flat conversion — 8 or 13 weeks.
Prior approval
Larger home extension / Class MA — 42 or 56 day clock.
Lawful Development Certificate
Confirm Permitted Development rights — 8 weeks.

Phase 1
Preparation
Measured survey of your property, existing drawings produced, proposed drawings designed, design & access statement written, supporting documents assembled.
1–2 weeks
Phase 2
Validation
Application submitted to the Planning Portal. Council checks the submission is complete, fees paid, and all required documents attached before officially registering it.
1–2 weeks
Phase 3
Consultation
Statutory 21-day neighbour and public consultation. Site notice posted, neighbours formally notified, statutory consultees (Highways, Heritage, Trees etc.) asked for comments.
3–4 weeks
Phase 4
Decision
Case officer reviews objections, visits site, drafts a recommendation, and either issues a delegated decision or reports to planning committee.
2–4 weeks
