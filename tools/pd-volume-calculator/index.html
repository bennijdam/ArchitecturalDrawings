Is My Loft Conversion Within Permitted Development Limits?

Under Class B of the GPDO, loft additions up to 40m³ (terraced) or 50m³ (detached / semi-detached) can be built without planning permission — if every other Class B rule is also met.

What type of property is it?

Your property type determines your Class B volume allowance.

Class B allowance: 40m³ for terraced and end-of-terrace houses; 50m³ for detached and semi-detached houses. Flats and maisonettes are not eligible for loft PD rights under Class B.

Step 3 of 4

Has any previous loft or roof addition been made?

Class B allowance is cumulative — any previous loft/roof additions (including by previous owners, since 1948) count against your 40m³/50m³ limit.
